🎲 Campaign Style

A balanced mix of roleplay, combat, and exploration. Perfect for both new and experienced players!

📅 Schedule

Weekly sessions every Saturday evening, 6–9 PM. Sessions typically run 3–4 hours with breaks.

🗺️ Setting

The war-torn continent of Eldoria, where ancient magic and political intrigue shape the fate of nations.

⚔️ Level Range

Starting at Level 3, with potential to reach Level 15–20. Characters with established backstories welcomed!

About the Campaign

Welcome, brave adventurer! The Shattered Realms campaign takes place in a world recovering from the Cataclysm — a magical disaster that tore the continent of Eldoria into fragments. Ancient kingdoms have fallen, new powers have risen, and the very fabric of reality remains unstable.

This campaign welcomes players of all experience levels. New to D&D? We'll teach you everything you need to know. Veteran player? Bring your creativity and let's tell an epic story together!
