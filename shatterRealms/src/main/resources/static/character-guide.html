Character Creation Guide

Getting Started

Characters start at Level 3 using standard 5th Edition D&D rules. You may use content from the Player's Handbook, Xanathar's Guide, and Tasha's Cauldron with DM approval.

Ability Scores: Standard array (15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8) or point buy.

Starting Equipment: Standard equipment for your class, plus one uncommon magic item or 500 gold pieces.

Need inspiration? Check out our world lore to understand the setting better.

Recommended Character Concepts

The Survivor

Any Race | Fighter / Ranger / Rogue

Someone who lived through the Cataclysm and has spent years surviving in the shattered lands. You've seen the worst of what the world has to offer and have the scars to prove it.

The Scholar

Human / Elf / Gnome

A researcher studying the Cataclysm and seeking to understand or perhaps undo its effects. Your knowledge of ancient magic could be the key to saving the world.

The Noble

Paladin / Bard / Sorcerer

Heir to a fallen kingdom or representative of a new power. You seek to restore your family's honor or carve out a new legacy in this changed world.

The Outcast

Tiefling / Half-Orc / Dragonborn

Society has never fully accepted you, but in this chaotic new world, perhaps you can finally find your place or burn down the old order entirely.

The Guardian

Dwarf / Goliath / Firbolg

Protector of the natural world or a sacred site affected by the Cataclysm. You adventure to heal the land and defend those who cannot defend themselves.

The Seeker

Any Race

You're searching for something a lost loved one, a legendary artifact, answers to questions that haunt you. The Shattered Realms hold many secrets, and you'll find yours.

Character Backgrounds

Consider how your character relates to the Cataclysm:

  • Were you there when it happened? Did you lose someone?
  • Were you born after, in the broken world?
  • Do you seek to restore the old world or embrace the new?
  • What drives you to adventure rather than simply survive?
